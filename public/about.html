Understanding Career Roadmaps

A career roadmap is your personalized guide from where you are now to where you want to be. Each roadmap includes:

  • Essential Skills: Core competencies you need to master
  • Hands-on Projects: Practical work to build your portfolio
  • Learning Sequence: Step-by-step progression from basics to advanced
  • Tools & Technologies: Industry-standard tools you should know
  • Career Stages: Clear milestones from entry-level to expert

By following your roadmap and marking tasks complete, you transform abstract career goals into concrete achievements.
